<h4>Executive Summary</h4>

This memo presents a synthesis of the performance of the Phi-3 series on the MMLU (Multilingual, Multimodal, and Long-context Understanding) tasks. The Phi-3 series, including the Phi-3.5-mini, Phi-3.5-MoE, and Phi-3.5-Vision models, have demonstrated significant improvements in language reasoning, math, and code tasks compared to other open-source models of similar scale [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0001]. However, there are some performance drops observed when testing the 128K context window on certain tasks [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0018].

<h4>Key Findings</h4>

<h5>Performance of Phi-3 Models on MMLU</h5>

The Phi-3.5-mini and Phi-3.5-MoE models outperform other open-source models with larger sizes, such as Llama-3.1-8B, Mixtral-8x7B, and Mixtral-8x22B, on the RepoQA task. They also achieve comparable performance to Llama-3.1-8B on the RULER task [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0018]. 

The Phi-3.5-mini model achieves performance comparable to much larger models like Mistral-Nemo-12B and Llama-3.1-8B. The Phi-3.5-MoE model significantly outperforms other open-source models, offers performance comparable to Gemini-1.5 Flash, and achieves above 90% of the average performance of GPT-4o-mini across various language benchmarks [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0019].

<h5>Performance of Phi-3.5-Vision on MM RAI Benchmarks</h5>

The Phi-3.5-Vision model demonstrates enhanced performance across all RAI benchmarks, indicating that safety post-training notably enhances the RAI performance [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0037].

<h5>Performance Drop in 128K Context Window</h5>

A significant performance drop is observed when testing the 128K context window on the RULER task. This suggests that while the Phi-3 models excel in certain areas, there are still some limitations to their performance [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0018].

<h4>Conclusions</h4>

The Phi-3 series demonstrates superior performance on MMLU tasks compared to other open-source models of similar scale. However, there are areas, such as the 128K context window on the RULER task, where performance drops are observed. These findings suggest that while the Phi-3 series exhibits significant advancements in language reasoning, math, and code tasks, there are still areas for improvement.
