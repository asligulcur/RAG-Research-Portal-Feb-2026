## Research Team (2025). "Length-MAX Tokenizer for Language Models". *arXiv*.

**Key Claim:** The length-weighted objective reduces TPC relative to frequency-only baselines across all tested configurations. Length-MAX reduces TPC by 14-18% compared to BPE.
**Method:** The study measures intrinsic compression metrics across vocabulary sizes and domains.
**Limitations:** Limitations not stated in excerpt.
**Why it matters:** This source provides an approach to model compression that focuses on tokenization efficiency, specifically using a length-weighted objective. It extends the discussion on model compression by introducing a new metric (TPC) and a method (Length-MAX) that can reduce it.
