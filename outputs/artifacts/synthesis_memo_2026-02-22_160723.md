<h4>Executive Summary</h4>

This memo provides a synthesis of the performance of the Phi-3 series models on the MMLU (Multimodal Multilingual Understanding) tasks. The Phi-3 series, which includes phi-3.5-mini, phi-3.5-MoE, and phi-3.5-Vision, has demonstrated superior performance in language reasoning, math, and code tasks compared to other open-source models of similar scale [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0001]. The memo also discusses the performance of these models in comparison with other models and the improvements achieved over previous versions.

<h4>Key Findings</h4>

<h5>Performance of Phi-3 Models on MMLU Tasks</h5>

The Phi-3 series models have shown significant performance on MMLU tasks. The phi-3.5-MoE model, a 16 x 3.8B MoE model with 6.6 billion active parameters, has achieved superior performance in language reasoning, math, and code tasks compared to other open-source models of similar scale [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0001]. 

The phi-3.5-mini model has demonstrated significant improvement over the phi-3-mini model in languages [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0017]. The phi-3.5-MoE model significantly outperforms other open-source models, offers performance comparable to Gemini-1.5 Flash, and achieves above 90% of the average performance of GPT-4o-mini across various language benchmarks [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0019].

<h5>Comparison with Other Models</h5>

The Phi-3 series models have shown comparable or superior performance to other models. For instance, the phi-3.5-mini model achieves performance comparable to much larger models like Mistral-Nemo-12B and Llama-3.1-8B [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0019]. 

On the MMLU (5-Shot) benchmark, the Phi-3-mini model scored 68.8, while the Phi-3-small and Phi-3-medium models scored 75.7 and 78.0 respectively [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0014]. 

<h5>Improvements Over Previous Versions</h5>

The Phi-3 series models have shown significant improvements over their previous versions. The phi-3.5-mini model demonstrates significant improvement over the phi-3-mini model in languages [Source: Phi3_2024, Chunk: Phi3_2024_chunk_0017]. 

<h4>Conclusions</h4>

The Phi-3 series models, including phi-3.5-mini, phi-3.5-MoE, and phi-3.5-Vision, have demonstrated superior performance on MMLU tasks compared to other open-source models of similar scale. These models have also shown significant improvements over their previous versions, indicating the effectiveness of the updates and enhancements made to these models.
